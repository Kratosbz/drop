Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions?

What is RIPPLE?

RIPPLE is a daily token reward app where millions of people earn NXS tokens just by logging in, completing tasks, and referring friends. Your daily presence earns you real, on-chain rewards — no trading, no purchases required.

How does RIPPLE work?

Download the app, connect your wallet, and claim your daily reward. Your streak multiplier increases every consecutive day — up to 5×. Refer friends and earn 10% of everything they collect, forever. Complete daily tasks for bonus NXS drops.

What are NXS Points?

NXS Points track your contributions to the RIPPLE network. They come in two forms: Uptime Points, earned for daily check-ins and referrals; and Activity Points, earned when you complete tasks and milestones. Together they determine your NXS Token rewards.

What are NXS Tokens?

NXS Tokens are the rewards you earn for your daily participation in the RIPPLE network. They reflect your engagement and give you ownership stake in the ecosystem. Hold them, trade them, or stake them inside the app for additional passive income.

When and how are NXS Tokens awarded?

Daily rewards are credited to your in-app balance instantly upon claiming. Referral bonuses are distributed within 24 hours of your referral's activity. Token airdrops to on-chain wallets occur at intervals announced on our official channels.

Who can receive NXS Tokens?

RIPPLE is available globally. Some jurisdictions subject to sanctions may have limited eligibility for on-chain token distributions. Check our Terms & Conditions for the full list. All users can earn in-app NXS points regardless of location.
